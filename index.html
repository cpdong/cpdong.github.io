I am a PhD candidate of Bioinformatics in the Department of BioHealth Informatics at the IU School of Informatics and Computing(SOIC). I work with Dr. Yunlong Liu in the Center for Computational Biology and Bioinformatics at Indiana University School of Medicine(IUSM). I received BS degree from Jilin University, and a Master degree from Fudan University. I also interned in combio fields of genomics analysis, transcriptional regulation and bio database development.


Research

My research focuses on developing statistical and informatics methods for analyzing of multi-omics data. Projects include but not limited to

  • Splicing derived neoanitgen for cancer vaccine

  • Transcription regulation in cancer genomics

  • Alzheimer's disease study
